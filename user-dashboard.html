Brewing Instructions

1
Warm the Chawan: Pour hot water into your bowl, swirl it, and pour out. Wipe dry. This prevents the cold bowl from instantly lowering the tea temp.
2
Sift the Matcha: Scoop 2 scoops of powder (approx. 2g) through a fine metal sifter. This removes any small lumps, helping it dissolve smoothly.
3
Pour Water: Add 70ml of water heated to exactly 80°C. Boiling water ruins the L-theanine, making the tea taste overly bitter.
4
Whisk Vigorously: Use the bamboo whisk. Keep it floating above the bottom of the bowl and whisk with back-and-forth wrist movements for 30s until frothy.
