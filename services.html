Tea Experiences

GUIDED JOURNEYS

Specialty Tasting Sessions

Experience a curated flight of our rarest single-origin matchas.

The Uji Flight

A 45-minute guided tasting exploring three distinct expressions of Uji matcha. Learn to identify the subtle differences in umami, sweetness, and astringency derived from varying shading techniques and soil compositions.

TRADITION

Japanese Tea Ceremony

Immerse yourself in a full, traditional Chado experience. Our certified tea masters will guide you through the intricate etiquette and profound philosophy of harmony, respect, purity, and tranquility.

EXPERT GUIDANCE

Tea Master Consultations

Private 1-on-1 consultations for true tea scholars. Refine your whisking technique, learn advanced water temperature control, or get tailored recommendations for pairing matcha with traditional wagashi (sweets).
