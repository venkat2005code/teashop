COMMON INQUIRIES

Frequently Asked Questions

Find immediate answers regarding shipping, storage, and workshop preparations.

How should I store my ceremonial matcha?

To preserve the vibrant green color and delicate flavor, seal the tin tightly and store it in the refrigerator. Consume within 6 to 8 weeks after opening for peak freshness.

Can I change or cancel my monthly delivery?

Yes, you can pause, skip, or modify your subscription tiers anytime via the User Dashboard under the "Matcha Deliveries" section, or by reaching out to our support team.

What should I prepare for a virtual tea workshop?

Your Ceremony Kit contains everything needed: matcha, a bamboo whisk (Chasen), and a scoop (Chashaku). You only need to prepare hot water (approx. 80°C/176°F) and a wide bowl.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship our fresh stone-ground matcha worldwide directly from Kyoto using temperature-controlled express packaging to ensure optimal quality is maintained during transit.

VISITING US

Tea Room Etiquette

To maintain the serene atmosphere of Chado (The Way of Tea), we kindly ask our guests to observe these traditional guidelines.

No Fragrances

Please refrain from wearing strong perfumes or colognes, as they interfere with the delicate aroma of the matcha.

Clean Socks

Please wear clean white socks when stepping onto the traditional tatami mats to preserve the delicate straw fibers.

Quiet Reflection

Speak in quiet, gentle tones to allow yourself and others to fully immerse in the meditative quality of the ceremony.

Remove Jewelry

Remove rings, watches, and bracelets before handling the historic, handmade tea bowls to prevent scratching.
