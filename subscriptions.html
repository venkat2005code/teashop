LEARN FROM HOME

Virtual Japanese Tea Ceremonies

We believe barriers shouldn't block tradition. Our live online workshops bring the tea room to you. Each ticket includes a shipped "Ceremony Kit" containing premium matcha, an introductory chasen whisk, and instructions.

Under the guidance of our tea instructors, you'll learn the setup of the chawan, proper folding of the tea cloth (chakin), temperature control, and the delicate kinetics of whisking.

MINDFUL OFFICES

Matcha Breaks for Corporate Wellness

Replace the chaotic coffee run with a serene tea break. We design customized stress-relief programs for corporate offices. These 45-minute live or virtual sessions combine breathing exercises, a mindful brewing demonstration, and a guided tea-drinking session.

Studies indicate that the L-theanine in matcha relaxes the nervous system while increasing alpha brain waves, creating a state of alert calm—perfect for focus and productivity.
